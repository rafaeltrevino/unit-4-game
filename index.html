Rules

You’ve been good all year, so Santa has a special gift for you. If you can match your Shopping Cart to your Santa Dollars, then you can take the full amount home! Each Christmas item below has a hidden value. Click on each Christmas item to put it in your Shopping Cart. Use the Shopping Cart amount to help you figure out the value of each item.

When your Shopping Cart amount matches the Santa Dollars, you win! But watch out—if you go over the amount, the value gets deducted from your account!

Good luck!
